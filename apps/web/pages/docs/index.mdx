# Introduction
 
Welcome to VanyaJS! This is a simple introduction to Vanya and the incredible things you can do with it in your own project :)

## What is VanyaJS?

A **simple**, **powerful** and **robust** library for building sites that have a WOW expression on your users.

Ever been to [Awwwards](https://awwwards.com) and wonder how you could build those cool elements into your site? With Vanya, you can!
